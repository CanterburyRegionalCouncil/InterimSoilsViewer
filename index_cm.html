Interim Soil Layer
 

Soil mapping for the parts of Canterbury that do not have S‑map data and are not part of the Department of Conservation estate.

The Land Resource Inventory (LRI) has been improved by altering the polygon linework to better match the landscape; soil labels have been corrected and matched to a representative S‑map sibling.

Clicking on an area of the map where there are soil data () will retrieve soil information about that area, displaying the resultant information in a table below the map. Clicking on the icon, or anywhere in the row will download a factsheet.

Please see Help section for further detail about how to operate the site, in particular the map controls. The About and Provenance sections contain information regarding the background and methods used to create the data.
